Expectation vs Reality of College Life

Expectation:

  • A lot of freedom and independence
  • Endless parties and social events
  • Easily making a lot of new friends
  • Plenty of free time to enjoy hobbies and interests
  • Expected a large, green, and lush campus in college.
  • Bad quality of mess food
  • Good sports facilities
  • Classes are expected to be engaging and provide hands-on, practical exposure.

Reality:

  • Balancing responsibilities and freedom can be challenging
  • Very small Social Events
  • Making lasting friendships takes time and effort
  • Very hectic schedule with classes, assignments and exams
  • No permanent campus (small rented campus)
  • Not-so-favorable gender ratio
  • Average quality of mess food
  • Bad sport facilities
  • Classes feel like school classes, lacking practical exposure
